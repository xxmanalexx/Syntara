Privacy Policy

Effective: April 6, 2026

Syntara ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Syntara social media management platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and authentication details when you sign up.
  • Social Media Data: When you connect your Instagram or other social accounts, we access public profile information and content you authorize.
  • Usage Data: Content you create, drafts, analytics, and interaction data within the platform.
  • Communications: Messages and conversations managed through our inbox feature.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the Syntara service
  • To manage your social media accounts and publish content on your behalf
  • To sync analytics and insights from connected platforms
  • To communicate with you about your account and support requests

3. Data Storage & Security

We store data on secure cloud infrastructure. Your social media access tokens are encrypted using AES-256-GCM. You can disconnect your accounts at any time, which removes your social account connections.

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with Instagram (Meta), and other social platforms. Their use of your data is governed by their respective privacy policies.

5. Your Rights

You may access, update, or delete your account data at any time through your account settings or by contacting us. For data export or deletion requests, email privacy@syntara.io.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.
