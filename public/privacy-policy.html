Privacy Policy

Effective: April 6, 2026

Syntara ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Syntara social media management platform available at syntara.io. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and authentication details when you sign up.
  • Social Media Data: When you connect your Instagram or other social accounts, we access public profile information and content you explicitly authorize.
  • Usage Data: Content you create, drafts, analytics, and interaction data within the platform.
  • Communications: Messages and conversations managed through our inbox feature.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the Syntara service
  • To manage your social media accounts and publish content on your behalf
  • To sync analytics and insights from connected platforms
  • To communicate with you about your account and support requests

3. AI Message Processing

When you use our AI-powered inbox and automation features, message content may be processed locally on our servers or through AI models to categorize, route, respond to, or analyze your conversations. We do not use your message content to train third-party AI models. Message data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the service and is deleted upon account deletion or data export request.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained for the duration of your active subscription. Upon account deletion, we delete or anonymize your data within 30 days, except where legal obligations require longer retention (e.g., financial records). Social account connections are removed immediately upon disconnection.

5. Data Storage & Security

We store data on secure cloud infrastructure. Your social media access tokens are encrypted using AES-256-GCM. You can disconnect your accounts at any time, which removes your social account connections and associated access tokens.

6. Data Security & Breach Notification

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights, we will notify you via email within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

7. Third-Party Services

We integrate with Instagram (Meta), and other social platforms. Their use of your data is governed by their respective privacy policies. We encourage you to review those policies.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Object to processing of your data in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, email xmanalex1@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. GDPR Compliance (EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation, including the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. We process your data lawfully, on valid legal grounds, and only for the purposes described in this policy.

10. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. No tracking or advertising cookies are used. You can disable cookies through your browser, but some features may not function properly.

11. Children's Privacy

Syntara is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
